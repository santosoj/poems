title: «the·horned·god’s·gift»

description: this was written while editing video for a soon to be released
art project around «la nuit où laurier gaudreault s’est réveillé».
~~~~~~~~~~~~~

it's a quality of the witch
that everybody falls in love with her

everybody. including other witches.
and that…

two witches, i mean,
or more, or in fact, all of them

must be one of the world's most beautiful things

it is the horned god's gift
to those who deserve
the horned god's love

baal‑lucifer the hermaphrodite
whom they have worshipped in days of old
and whom they worship now

when they gather
they become each other's altar
before the great hermaphrodite

and excrement becomes sacrament
and nightshade becomes sacrament
